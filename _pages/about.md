I received the B.S. degree in Music Information Science from the University of Milan, Milan, Italy, in 2014, the M.S. degree in Computer Science and Engineering in 2018 and the Ph.D. (cum laude) in Information Engineering in 2022, both from Politecnico di Milano, Milan, Italy,  where he is currently working toward as a postdoc researcher in the Image and Sound Processing Lab within the Dipartimento di Elettronica, Informazione and Bioingegneria.

His main research interests concern the application of Deep Learning techniques to space-time audio signal problems, the development of techniques for Networked Music Performances and music generation.
He has been involved as a researcher for the INTERMusic project and as a teaching assistant in the courses of Musical Acoustics and Creative Programming and Computing of the M.Sc. in Music and Acoustic Engineering.
